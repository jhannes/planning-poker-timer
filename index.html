Presentation Countdown Timer

10 MINUTE TALK

Make the colours start at green, go to red when there's 30 seconds left and black when time's up.

5 MINUTE TALK

Make the colours start at green, go to red when there's 30 seconds left and black when time's up.

TEST

Test that the colors, audio and restart works. Starts at 10 seconds, changes colors at 5 and restarts at -5.

FULL SCREEN

Make the text bigger to fill the whole screen. This is CMD+ or CTRL+ in most browsers - or via the menu if you're using IE.

RESTART

Hitting the spacebar will also restart the timer (and make all of this text go away).

FEATURES

Bell: Rings a bell and turns the screen red (unless you specify other colours) when time is up.
Transitions: Change colors at specified times.
Restart: Restart specified times.
